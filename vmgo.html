VMGO

VISION

NVSU as a Premier University that nourishes a culture of excellence and peace.


MISSION

To deliver and produce competent human resources through high quality and relevant intruction, research, extension programs and entreprenuership for sustainable development of society.


GOALS

Academic Programs

To adapt and address itself as a functional learning center for innovative programs as a model institution and enabling its graduates to be empowered globally competitive through the provision of relevant undergraduate and graduate education which meet international standards of quality and excellence.

Research, Development and Extension Program

To generate, verify, package and appropriate technologies and create impact on the life of the clients.

University Business Affairs Program

To interface the academic, research, development and extension and administrative systems and services for viable income generating projects/livelihood program.

Administrative and Support Services

To provide adequate support for a productive and secure learning environment.

Goals (College of Engineering)

To model a learning center of engineering for agro-industrial and technical skills advancement


OBJECTIVES

College of Engineering

  • To offer the quality instraction on engineering that will enable the student to attain the necded competencies for agro-industrial and technical advancement.

  • To promoto research activities among faculties and students aimed to complementing classroom instruction.

  • To promote extension activities by developing adopting and promoting agro-industrial and technical advancement, through student ond faculty, in community development.

  • To establish income generating projects which can serve as a model for entrepreneurship.

  • To provide students wider access to engincering education through scholarship or assistance to under privileged but academically deserving students.

Agricultural Engineering

  1. Train students in the application of engineering principles particulary in the solution of problems related to agro-industriel development;

  2. Prepare them to become professional with entyr - level competencies;

  3. Develop appreciation in the students, of the potentials of an agricultural engineering business enterprise;

  4. Instill in the students a concern for the preservation and protection of the natural environment;

  5. Prepare students for advanced studies.

Geodetic Engineering

To produce Geodetic Engineers who are globally competiive, capable of taking lead roles in sustainable development projects. geodetic engincering related projects & researches, equipped with deep sense of social responsibility & environmental awareness.

Civil Engineering

To prepare the graduates for professional engineering career including a leading role in infrastructure planning and development. To process linkages of laws, contracts, resource management, research and extension and the protection of the environment. The graduates must have strong foundation in the physical sciences and should consider this econimic, social and environmental significance of what he/she plans to build.
